Regulatory
Compliance

Cybersecurity regulation has moved from voluntary frameworks to hard enforcement. SUDOLAW advises on compliance program development, regulatory examinations, and the legal dimensions of security policy across major frameworks.

Overview

Regulators across financial services, healthcare, and general commerce have significantly expanded their cybersecurity expectations and enforcement activity. Building a defensible compliance program requires understanding not just what the rules require, but how regulators interpret and apply them in examinations and enforcement proceedings. SUDOLAW advises organizations on both dimensions — helping build programs that satisfy legal requirements and hold up under scrutiny.

What We Do

  • NYDFS 23 NYCRR Part 500 — policy and procedure drafting, CISO reporting, annual certification support
  • HIPAA Security Rule — risk analysis, policy development, workforce training frameworks, BAA review
  • GLBA Safeguards Rule — written information security program development and review
  • FTC Safeguards Rule — compliance programs for non-banking financial institutions
  • SEC cybersecurity disclosure rule counseling and materiality analysis
  • State breach notification law analysis across all 50 states and D.C.
  • Regulatory examination preparation and response
  • Board and senior management cybersecurity reporting
  • Gap analysis against applicable regulatory requirements
