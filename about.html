The Practice

SUDOLAW was founded on a straightforward premise: organizations navigating serious cybersecurity risk deserve legal counsel that actually understands the technical landscape — how an incident response engagement runs, what a red team authorization needs to accomplish, and what regulators are actually looking for in an examination.

The practice draws on direct in-house experience supporting InfoSec departments, negotiating vendor contracts with security-aware precision, structuring offensive security authorizations, and advising on regulatory compliance programs across multiple jurisdictions. That background means clients do not spend time explaining their stack or their threat model — they get substantive legal advice informed by operational reality.
