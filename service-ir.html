Incident Response
Legal Support

When a breach occurs, the legal clock starts immediately. SUDOLAW provides counsel to direct the response, manage regulatory obligations, and protect the organization through investigation and recovery.

Overview

A cybersecurity incident triggers a cascade of legal obligations — breach notification deadlines, regulatory reporting windows, potential litigation exposure, and insurance coverage questions — all running simultaneously with the technical response. SUDOLAW coordinates the legal track of incident response so that decisions are made with full awareness of their legal consequences in real time.

What We Do

  • Legal direction and oversight of forensic IR teams
  • Breach counsel and regulatory notification strategy across state, federal, and international jurisdictions
  • Root cause analysis documentation and findings management
  • Vendor and third-party breach coordination
  • Regulator-facing communications and response letters
  • Cyber insurance coverage analysis and claim support
  • Board and executive briefing on legal exposure
  • Post-incident remediation guidance and regulatory follow-up

Notification Jurisdiction Coverage

Data breach notification obligations vary significantly across jurisdictions — timing windows, covered data types, regulatory recipients, and required content differ by state and country. SUDOLAW analyzes notification obligations across all applicable jurisdictions, drafts required notices, and manages the notification process from start to finish.
